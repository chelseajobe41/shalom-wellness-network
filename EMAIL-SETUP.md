# Get Form Emails to Your Inbox

Your website has three forms (Contact, Quiz, Free Guide). Every time someone fills one out, the answers should land in your Gmail at `mamamboga4life@gmail.com`. To turn that on, you do **one thing, one time**.

---

## Do This

### 1. Open your live website on your phone or computer.

### 2. Go to the **Contact** page (top menu) and fill out the form.

Use your real info. First name, last name, your email, your phone, your state, and a short note like "Testing the form." Hit **Request My Free 15-Min Call**.

### 3. Open Gmail at `mamamboga4life@gmail.com`.

Within a minute or two, you'll see a new email. It looks like this:

> **From:** FormSubmit <noreply@formsubmit.co>
> **Subject:** Confirm your email
>
> Hi! Someone is using FormSubmit to send you form submissions. Please click the button below to start receiving them.
>
> **[ Activate ]**

If you don't see it, **check Spam or Promotions** (the first one sometimes lands there).

### 4. Click the **Activate** button in that email.

A page will load saying "You're activated!" That's it.

---

## You're Done

From now on, every Contact form, every Quiz submission, and every Free Guide signup gets emailed to you automatically. You will never have to click Activate again.

---

## What You'll Get

Every form submission arrives as a clean, formatted email:

| Form | What's in the email |
|---|---|
| **Contact** | Their name, email, phone, state, what made them reach out, how they heard about you |
| **Metabolic Quiz** | Their name, email, their result zone (In Balance / Stressed / Inflamed), score (e.g., 14 of 33), and all 10 of their quiz answers in plain English |
| **Quiz · "Book My Free 15-Min Call"** | If a quiz-taker also clicks the call button at the end, a second email arrives with subject "⏰ Quiz visitor wants a 15-min call" |
| **Free Guide** | Their name, email, which guide they requested |

Subject lines start with words like "New consultation request" or "New quiz submission" so you can spot them at a glance.

---

## If Something's Off

**No FormSubmit email arrived.**
Wait 5 minutes, then check Spam, Promotions, and Updates folders. If still nothing, submit the form again.

**I activated it but emails aren't coming in.**
Test by filling out the Contact form again. The first email after activation sometimes takes 1 to 2 minutes. After that, they're instant.

**The form said "Sorry, something went wrong."**
This usually means the visitor was on a weak connection. The form falls back to showing them your email (`mamamboga4life@gmail.com`) so they can reach you directly. You'll know to expect them.
