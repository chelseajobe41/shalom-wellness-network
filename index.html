The Three Components

Unimate , Yerba Mate Concentrate

Unimate supports energy, appetite regulation, and mood , without the spike and crash of stimulants. It's taken in the morning, during your fasting window, to extend the fast comfortably while keeping your mind sharp and your hunger manageable.

What it addresses: Energy depletion, mental fog, appetite dysregulation, mood instability.

Balance , Fiber Matrix Complex

Balance is taken before meals to blunt the blood sugar response to food. It creates a physical fiber matrix in the gut that slows glucose absorption , reducing the insulin spike that drives fat storage, cravings, and energy crashes.

What it addresses: Post-meal glucose spikes, insulin overproduction, cravings, fat storage.

Intermittent Fasting , The Amplifier

The fasting window isn't optional , it's where the protocol's power compounds. Combined with Unimate and Balance, strategic fasting allows the body to lower baseline insulin levels, reduce inflammation, and begin the metabolic reset that diet alone rarely achieves.

What it addresses: Elevated fasting insulin, chronic inflammation, metabolic stagnation.

STORY · STROKE RECOVERY

Ruth & Jonathan

"You no longer need cholesterol medication. You no longer need Metformin. You no longer need half of your blood pressure medication."

After suffering a major stroke in 2024 and another in 2025, Ruth's quality of life had drastically declined. Her mobility was severely affected. Even simple daily activities took tremendous effort. Most days, all she could manage was getting out of bed to retrieve what she needed before returning to rest. She struggled with fatigue, poor sleep, weight gain, and very low energy.

Everything began to change after starting the Feel Great System.

Almost immediately, Ruth noticed a surge in energy. Within just two weeks, she was walking more, moving around the house, standing longer, and slowly regaining strength and confidence.

About three months in, the transformation has been remarkable. Her A1C is normal. Her blood pressure is normal. Just one month into the journey, her physician reduced her medications by half. Then after her most recent lab work in April 2026, her doctor personally called with the news:

"Your cholesterol numbers are amazing. You no longer need cholesterol medication. Your A1C is normal. You no longer need Metformin. And you no longer need half of your blood pressure medication."

But perhaps the most emotional improvement has been Ruth's speech. After her strokes she struggled with dysphasia, with finding words and forming sentences. Today she speaks clearly, fluidly, and confidently. Her voice is strong and audible again.

She's back in church serving, standing as long as she wants, participating in activities she had not been able to do for nearly two years.

Then came the sweet surprise.

Her husband Jonathan had also suffered a major stroke in 2023. He had been undergoing physical therapy and had not driven for two full years, all of 2024 and 2025.

Two weeks ago, Ruth went to the train station expecting Jonathan to arrive with someone else driving. To her shock and joy, Jonathan was the one behind the wheel.

The protocol had strengthened him, increased his confidence, and helped him feel steady and capable again. After two years without driving, Jonathan drove himself.

Stories like Ruth and Jonathan's are why this work matters. Improved metabolic health can truly improve quality of life. Hope is possible. Strength can return. Life can begin again.
